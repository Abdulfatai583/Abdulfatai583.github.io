International FMCG Sales Analysis

Project Overview

This project analyzes international FMCG sales data using SQL to provide insights into profit trends, regional performance, and cost efficiency across multiple countries. It helps managers make strategic business decisions.

Dataset

  • COUNTRIES: Country of sale
  • YEARS: Year of sale
  • MONTHS: Month of sale
  • BRANDS: Product brand
  • PROFIT: Profit amount
  • QUANTITY: Quantity sold
  • REGION: Region in country
  • SALES_REP: Sales representative

Business Questions

  1. Total profit by territory over 3 years
  2. Comparison between Francophone and Anglophone countries
  3. Highest profit country in 2019
  4. Year with highest profit
  5. Month with least profit
  6. Minimum profit in December 2018
  7. Monthly profit percentages in 2019
  8. Brand with highest profit in Senegal

Key Insights

  • Francophone countries contributed a significant portion of total profit.
  • Nigeria had the highest beer consumption in West Africa.
  • Budweiser, Guinness, and Heineken dominate specific regions.
  • Seasonal trends identify months with lower profits for promotional opportunities.

Technologies Used
