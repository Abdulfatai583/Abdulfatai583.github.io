International FMCG Sales Analysis

Project Overview

This project analyzes international FMCG sales data using SQL to provide insights into profit trends, regional performance, and cost efficiency across multiple countries. It helps managers make informed strategic decisions.

Dataset

  • COUNTRIES: Country of sale
  • YEARS: Year of sale
  • MONTHS: Month of sale
  • BRANDS: Product brand
  • PROFIT: Profit amount
  • QUANTITY: Quantity sold
  • REGION: Region in country
  • SALES_REP: Sales representative

Business Questions Addressed

  1. Total profit by territory over the last three years
  2. Comparison of profits between Francophone and Anglophone countries
  3. Country with the highest profit in 2019
  4. Year with the highest profit
  5. Month with the least profit
  6. Minimum profit in December 2018
  7. Monthly profit percentages in 2019
  8. Brand with highest profit in Senegal
  9. Profit trends over months
  10. Top 3 brands consumed in Francophone countries (2018–2019)
  11. Top 2 consumer brands in Ghana
  12. Beer consumption in Nigeria by brand and year
  13. Favorite malt brands in Anglophone regions (2018–2019)
  14. Highest-selling brands in Nigeria in 2019
  15. Favorite brands in South-South Nigeria
  16. Beer consumption by brand over years in Nigeria
  17. Budweiser consumption by region in Nigeria
  18. Budweiser consumption by region in 2019 (for promo decisions)
  19. Country with highest beer consumption
  20. Top Budweiser salesperson in Senegal
  21. Country with highest profit in Q4 2019

Key Insights

  • Francophone countries contributed a significant portion of total profit over 2017–2019.
  • Nigeria consistently had the highest beer consumption in West Africa.
  • Popular brands such as Budweiser, Guinness, and Heineken dominate specific regions.
  • Seasonal trends highlight months with lower profit, providing opportunities for promotions.

Technologies Used

  • SQL: Data querying, aggregation, and analysis
  • HTML/CSS: Documentation formatting
